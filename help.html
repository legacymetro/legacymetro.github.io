AN INNOVATIVE REAL ESTATE COMPANY

Q: Are you a real estate agent and are there fees or commissions involved?

A: No! We are real estate investors who love helping people by buying and selling homes. There are never any fees or commissions involved when you work with us. We will also handle all the paperwork associated with buying or selling your house. Our goal is to make the buying and sales process a smooth, easy transaction for you. You will never have to pay us anything up front or during the sales process.

Q: What types of homes do you buy?

A: We buy all types of homes. Any size. Any condition. Any situation. You don't need to make any repairs, or even clean, before we buy your home. If you're in financial distress or just ready to move on to the next phase and having a hard time selling, we are confident that we can help you. Oftentimes we can make you a fair cash offer and close within 30 days. Just give us a call!

Q: What types of homes do you sell?

A: We sell all types of homes. We have everything from complete rehabs to beautiful move-in ready homes to rental properties. If you don't see what you're looking for in our current inventory, let us know. We have a large network of sellers, so we're confident that we can find exactly what you're looking for at just the right price. Just give us a call!

Q: Why are your homes priced so low?

A: As real estate investors we buy homes through many different scenarios. Many times we are able to negotiate directly with the banks and buy properties at well below market value. In turn, we pass those large savings on to you.
