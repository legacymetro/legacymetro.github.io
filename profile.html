About me
This year I celebrate 16 years in the Real Estate industry. I've been happily married to a wonderful and supportive husband since 2001. Raising 5 beautiful children (well...4 children and one College student :D). We are on a mission to empower our families, strengthen our community and show others how to do the same.

One of the most important concepts I learned as a child was that a positive philosophy resulted in a positive attitude, which resulted in positive action. As an adult I have really embraced the philosophy and I agree that money only accentuates the person you are inside. It is very easy to let greed, and unethical practices consciously or subconsciously guide our actions, especially in the real estate business.

It is up to us to help guide our children and give them options. I believe that if we can prevent them from spending the balance of their lives at someone elses j.o.b. (just over broke) we have given them a tremendous head start and solid foundation.

If you are interested in coaching/mentoring visit my website www.reiknowledge.com
